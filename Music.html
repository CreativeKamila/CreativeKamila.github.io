Music!


OWAA AAA AA AAA!!! Here's where you can find all sorts of music that I'm a fan of, ranging from heavy metal to cutsey kpop

Metal

I love metal because I'm a huge fan of quick paced music that fills me with energy, and metal does just that. I've been into metal music for a decent amount of time now, diving more into the classics of metal than I did in the past. However, I do prefer the more experimental nature and blend of genres that modern metal has to offer. I also really enjoy learning about the different subgenres and learning more about the metalhead subculture in general. My favourite metal bands are Bring Me the Horizon (Metalcore/Numetal), Slipknot (Numetal), and Conquer Divide (Metalcore)

Pop

I love any type of music I can dance to, anything groovy, anything a wee bit wibey, if you will. Pop music has always been a part of my life and it always will be, I can't help but enjoy a good pop bop every now and again! My favourite pop artists are Marina, IDKHBTFM, and Ashnikko

International

I love music from around the world, no matter the genre. I especially love Kpop (Korean pop) and Jrock (Japanese rock). I love learning the words to songs in other languages, I find it to be a fun challenge. With Japanese, I can understand and read a decent amount of the lyrics which definitely makes it easier to sing along. Latin languages like Spanish and French are also easy to sing along to. I find the most challenging language to sing along to is Norwegian, but I think it sounds super cool!!! My favourite international artists are Stray Kids (Kpop), Ado (Jrock), Måneskin (Italian rock)

Alternative Rock

I adore alt rock, I've been listening to it since childhood, and I still do to this day. Unlike with metal, it's more chill than it is aggressive, so it's definitely easier for most people to get into. My favourite part of rock is the sound of the guitars and vocals. Nothing makes me more energetic than hearing a strong vocal, it brings me so much joy. My favourite alt rock bands are Fall Out Boy, and Gorillaz

Soundtracks

From movies, to musicals, to video games, I. Love. Soundtracks! The way that music can take you into a whole new world (not an Aladdin reference, I swear) is absolutely breathtaking. I love soundtracks that have a whole variety of songs that fit the vibe of each scene perfectly. I especially love when original soundtracks create themes for characters, so that even if a character isn't on screen, you KNOW they're about to show up and do something cool (Spiderman 2099, anyone?). My favourite soundtracks are the Persona 5 sountrack, the Baldur's Gate Three soundtrack (shocker), and the Hamilton soundtrack (yes, I'm a theatre kid, can't you tell?)

Covers

One thing I love just as much as original songs are covers. Seeing the way that different artists tackle the same song really shows how everybody is unique and how music is adaptable, ESPECIALLY when those cover artists genrebend. There's something special about people putting their own spin to songs that are already known and loved, sometimes the covers end up being even better than the original! My favourite cover artists (who also do original music) are Johnathan Young, Caleb Hyles and Lauren Babic
